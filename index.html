About Me

Hello, I am Connor. I am a current undergraduate student at St. John's University in Minnesota. I major in Computer Science, but my interests are more in parallel processing and the tools people can use to speed up their development process. This is a fascinating area regarding the widespread application of the results.

Moving away from computers, I am interested in sustainability and how we can improve code for people and the planet.

Throughout my academic journey, I've developed skills in various programming languages and frameworks, with a particular focus on creating efficient and sustainable code. I believe in the power of technology to solve real-world problems while minimizing environmental impact.

My goal is to continue exploring the intersection of high-performance computing and sustainability, finding ways to make our digital infrastructure more efficient and environmentally friendly.
